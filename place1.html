The Lazy Lake

Location: Near the old banyan tree, 2 km from Kishangarh city center.

The Lazy Lake is not in a hurry. It has never been in a hurry. If ambition were a body of water, this would not be it. The surface remains calm even when dramatic ducks land with unnecessary confidence.
